Last Updated: 2024-12-28

Welcome to BookVoyage ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our service to create personalized travel guides and stories for children ("Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data Provided by Users: We collect information you provide when creating an account or using our Service, such as your name, email address, and payment details (processed through third-party services like Stripe). We also collect optional data about the children’s names and specific travel/story details you provide for personalization.

b. Uploaded Images: If you choose to upload images of individuals for illustration in the personalized travel guide, we temporarily store these images and pass them to third-party services (e.g., DALL·E) for image generation.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and personalize the travel guides and stories using services such as OpenAI for text generation and DALL·E (or similar) for image generation.

  • To improve our Service, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • OpenAI / DALL·E: Your text input and uploaded images are shared with these services to generate content for your personalized book.

  • Printing and Shipping: We share necessary order details (such as name and shipping address) with Lulu to print and deliver your books.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at [Contact Email].

5. Security Measures

We employ reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no security system is impenetrable.

6. Children’s Privacy

This Service is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. Any information about children (such as names or trip details) is provided voluntarily by the adult user.
